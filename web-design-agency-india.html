FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?+

A standard business website typically takes 2–3 weeks from onboarding to launch. E-commerce stores take 3–5 weeks depending on catalogue size. Premium corporate builds with multiple pages and animations take 4–6 weeks. Timelines are confirmed at the project start and we deliver 100% on time.

Do you use templates or page builders?+

No. Every Vynclare website is designed and coded from scratch. We don't use Elementor, WPBakery, Divi, or pre-purchased themes. This is why our websites load faster, look distinctive, and perform better in search results.

Do you work with international businesses?+

Yes. We've built for clients in the UAE, USA, and India. The entire project process briefing, design review, revisions, delivery runs remotely. We're comfortable working across time zones and in different market contexts.

What do you need from us to get started?+

We start with a discovery call to understand your business, audience, and goals. After that, we need your brand assets (logo, colours if you have them), any content you want on the site, and examples of websites you like. We guide you through every step you don't need to prepare a detailed brief.

What happens after the website launches?+

Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support for any tweaks or issues. After that, monthly maintenance plans are available covering security, updates, speed optimisation, and ongoing SEO improvements.
